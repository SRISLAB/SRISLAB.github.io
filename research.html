Histological whole slide image analysis, 2017-2018

This project aims to develop an online application for histopathological whole slide image (WSI) analysis based on deep learning methods. The application helps pathologists locate lesion regions in WSIs. We have done preliminary studies on breast, cervix and lung cancer databases. A demo can be found on the Remote Cancer Diagnosis and Communication PlatForm - MoticGallery .

Content-based histological image retrieval, 2013-2018

We introduce content-based image retrieval (CBIR) apporachs into histological image analysis. It helps pathologicals understand a region of histological image through searching for similar regions from a database containing diagnosed whole slide images. It involves studies on color normalization, feature extraction, hashing methods for histological images, and specific retrieval schemes from databases that consist of whole slide images. This research is supported by National Natural Science Foundations of China (No. 61371134 and No. 61771031) and Motic-BUAA Image Technology Research and Development Center.
