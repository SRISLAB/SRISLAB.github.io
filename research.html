Manufacturing big data-driven rotating machinery interpreable fault diagnosis, 2023-2025

The "14th Five-Year Plan" Intelligent Manufacturing Development Plan regards "promoting the manufacturing industry to realize digital transformation, network collaboration, and intelligent transformation" as the main goal of the development of intelligent manufacturing. Discrete manufacturing has the characteristics of intensive knowledge and technology, long service life, and large value. The safe, reliable, intelligent, and efficient operation of its key equipment and production lines has become the focus of academic research and industrial applications. This project intends to be oriented to the needs of interpretable fault diagnosis in discrete manufacturing, centering on the two key scientific issues of "formal neural network design and formal reasoning" and "formal verification of interpretable fault diagnosis and industrial field verification", for manufacturing Big data-driven intelligent equipment interpretable fault diagnosis provides theories and methods to promote the intelligent transformation and upgrading of my country's discrete manufacturing industry and lay a theoretical and technical foundation for high-quality innovation and development. First, construct a formal neural network to infer a formal language for fault diagnosis and interpretation of intelligent equipment; second, study the formal knowledge transfer and information fusion method of fault mechanism to overcome the lack of typical sample data for fault diagnosis and the difficulty of system-level fault diagnosis Finally, a formal definition of interpretable fault diagnosis and a verification method are proposed, and the safety and effectiveness of the fault diagnosis algorithm are verified in the industrial field. Natural Science Foundations of Guangdong, China

Formal definition, verification and reasoning of interpretable fault diagnosis for offshore wind turbine, 2022-2024

Closely focusing on the national "double carbon" goal, this project intends to focus on the problem of insufficient research on explainable fault diagnosis of wind turbine drive chain, and study the basic scientific problems and practical engineering technology of explainable fault diagnosis of wind turbine drive chain based on formal language. Firstly, this project proposes to study the formal definition and verification methods of explainable fault diagnosis, and highlight the correlation between explainable fault diagnosis and fault mechanism. Secondly, the project proposes a formal language for wind turbine drive chain fault diagnosis, designs a formal neural network based on the formal language for formal reasoning and fault diagnosis, optimizes the structure of the formal neural network, and ensures that the neural network learning results are closely related to the fault mechanism. Again, on the basis of formal neural network, this project intends to study the knowledge transfer method of fault mechanism based on multi-adaptation, investigate the logical relationship between part-level fault mechanism and system-level fault mechanism, and apply the knowledge of part-level fault mechanism to system-level fault diagnosis and mechanism analysis through the formal information fusion method. Finally, this project intends to use the laboratory bearing gear experimental platform and industrial field data to carry out engineering application research and provide decision guidance and theoretical support for the safe operation and maintenance of wind turbines. This research is supported by Natural Science Foundations of Guangdong, China
