SRIS (Smart, Reliable, and Interpretable Systems) lab is a research group directed by Dr. Gang Chen at South China University of Technology. The main research interest includes machine learning, formal methods, control theory, signal processing and their applications on cyber-physical system monitoring and control.

Recent News


    [Aug 24, 2023] Our lab has received a new research grant from NSFC on "Research on interpretable and quantitative characterization method of fault evolution mechanism of wind turbine gearbox based on temporal logic".

    [July 6, 2023] Our paper " Evolution Strategies-Based Optimized Graph Reinforcement Learning for Solving Dynamic Job Shop Scheduling Problem" has been accepted by Applied Soft Computing.

    [June 29, 2023] One of the undergraduate dissertations in our laboratory was selected as the excellent undergraduate dissertation of the South China University of Technology.

    [Feb 7, 2023] Our lab has received a research grant from the Guangdong Basic and Applied Basic Research Fund on "Formal definition, verification and reasoning of interpretable fault diagnosis for offshore wind turbines".

    [March 11, 2022] Our paper "Interpretable Fault Diagnosis with Shapelet Temporal Logic: Theory and Application" has been accepted by Automatica.

    [Nov 11, 2022] Our lab has received a research grant from the Guangdong Basic and Applied Basic Research Fund on "Manufacturing Big Data-Driven Rotating Machinery interpreable Fault Diagnosis".
